Orchard Beach

Welcome!

Orchard beach is a New York City Public Beach located in Pelham Bay Bronx

It is the Bronx's only public beach. It sports 1.1-mile-long beach, a promanade, a central pavillion, snack bars, two playgrounds, two picnic areas, a large parking lot, and a lot more. Changing areas and showers are also available.

This is not only just for beach going but also for exploring. The vast park the beach is located in is full of surprises. You might be able to see deers, butterflies, and unique species of birds.

Come and explore Orchard Beach!
