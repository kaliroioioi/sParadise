Orchard Beach

Directions

By Public Transit:
Take the 6 train towards Pelham Bay Park. Get off on Pelham Bay Park. It is the last stop unless trains are rerouted.
Take the Bx29 towards City Island. Get off on the City Island/Orchard Circle. It is the a big rotund.
Now you must walk towards Park Dr. It is the road to the left of where the bus dropped you off accross the rotund.
Watch out for cars when crossing!
Also there are no sidewalks so keep on the bike/walk path next to Park Dr. In about 5-10 minutes of walking, you will see the Orchard Beach sign towards the right.
